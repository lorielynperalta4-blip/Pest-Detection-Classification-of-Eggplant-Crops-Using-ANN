About Our System

Our Eggplant Pest Detection System is powered by an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) — the core technology behind our capstone project at Isabela State University - San Mateo. This deep learning model mimics the human brain’s pattern recognition capability to accurately classify pests from images of eggplant leaves and stems.

We trained and deployed our ANN model using Roboflow, which provided the tools to preprocess field-collected images, augment data for robustness, and deploy a scalable inference API. The result is a practical tool that brings cutting-edge AI directly to farmers’ fingertips.

  • ANN-Powered Detection – Learns complex visual patterns to identify pests with high precision.
  • Real-Time Analysis – Get instant results via Roboflow’s cloud-hosted inference engine.
  • Smart Treatment Plans – Tailored chemical, biological, and cultural recommendations per pest.
  • Personal Pest Log – Track detections over time to monitor crop health and intervention effectiveness.

Designed specifically for smallholder farmers, our mobile-friendly web app requires minimal data and works reliably even on low-bandwidth connections — bringing university-grade AI research into real-world farming environments.
